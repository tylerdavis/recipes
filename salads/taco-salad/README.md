# Taco Salad

## Ingredients

### Salad

| Name | Quantity |
|------|---------:|
Boneless chicken thigh or breast | 1/2 lb
Lime chile rub | 1/2 cup
Red Onion | 1/2
Cherry Tomatoes | 10
Corn - shucked | 1 ear
Black beans | 1 cup
Cotija cheese | 1/2 cup |
Arugula | 1 bunch
Red pepper | 1
Lime | 1
Sour cream | 1/2 cup
Tortilla Chips | 2 cups
Pico de gayo | 1 cup

### Dressing
| Name | Quantity |
|------|---------:|
Avocado | 1
Lime | 1
Garlic | 2 cloves
Cilantro | 1/2 cup
Salt | 1 pinch
Pepper | 1 pinch
Olive Oil | 3 tablespoons
Water | To taste
Jalapeno - deseeded | 1/2

------------------------

## Instructions

1. Pat dry chicken, apply lime chile rub
2. Grill or pan fry the chicken
